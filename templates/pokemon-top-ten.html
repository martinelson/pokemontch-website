Not a lot of surprises in the ranking of most common pokemon printed. In the sample dataset, 8 out of the top 10 are from the original 150. It's also likely that Unown made the list due to the printing per letter of the alphabet. In comparing the most common card printed with the most valuable Pokemon printed on average, only two are in both lists, Charizard and Blastoise. Charizard by far is the most popular type of card in the game, with the shadowless holo being the most valuable card in the dataset.
