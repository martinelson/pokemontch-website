The most valuable set on average is the POP Series 5 set with an total average value of $171 across the sample dataset. It's worth mentioning that although the average value is this high, the expected value of each of these packs is much lower. With using the two most valuable cards in the set, the Espeon and Umbreon Gold Star cards, and an estimated pull rate for those two cards of 1 in 40 packs, the expected value for the pack just using these two cards as the baseline is closer to $55. With unopened packs of the POP Series 5 going for as much as $650 on eBay and elsewhere, these packs of cards are a very risky investment. If someone is wanting to get these two cards as a part of a collection, then it's more likely to be worth it to purchase those cards separately.
